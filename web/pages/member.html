    JONG WOOK KIM (M'17) received the Ph.D. degree from the Computer Science Department, Arizona State University, in 2009. He was a Software Engineer with the Query Optimization Group at Teradata, from 2010 to 2013. He is currently an Assistant Professor of computer science with Sangmyung University. His primary research interest is in the area of data privacy, distributed databases, and query optimization.
